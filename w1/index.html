Hi I'm Andrew Rockwell, and I'm a League of Legends nerd who also plays tennis and golf. I was always raised playing those 2 sports, but when around season 4 hit in League, my friend introduced me to the game and I became hooked. I used to try and rank up more in League, but realized around season 8 that I would rather play for fun than try and be the best. So just like tennis and golf, league became a leisure hobby that I enjoy a lot on the side instead of trying to become the best player around. For tennis and golf I learned around my first year of college that I did not want to play these sports competitively. I would rather enjoy a game I love than hate the game down the line.
