The Kingsman is a secret service centered around world protection against all who try and disturb the peace for personal gain. After the death of Agent Lancelot, the organization looks for a suitable replacement to take his place. Eggsy is a street kid who has been recruited in the competitive training program to become a spy for the organization. How does he match up vs the other candidates? What dangers are currently lurking out there?
